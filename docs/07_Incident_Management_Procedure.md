# Incident Management Procedure (v1.1)

**Version:** v1.1  
**Last updated:** 2026-01-20  
**Applies:** Internal operations

---

## 1) Purpose

Provide a consistent method to identify, record, investigate, and respond to incidents.

---

## 2) What counts as an incident

Includes:

- product failure/defect/safety issue
- delivery damage/missing/incorrect items
- alleged injury linked to product use
- suspected privacy breach
- repeated supplier failure patterns

---

## 3) Mandatory recording (register alignment)

Every incident must be recorded in **Incident_Register.md** the same day where possible.

If linked to an order, reference the matching **Order_Log.md** entry.

---

## 4) Triage severity

- **Low** / **Moderate** / **High**, based on risk of harm, scale, and customer impact.

---

## 5) Investigation steps

1. confirm facts (order record, supplier record, comms, photos)
2. isolate whether supplier/carrier/system contributed
3. decide remedy path (repair/replace/refund/support)
4. log corrective actions

---

## 6) Escalation

Escalate to supplier account manager and document their response in the supplier evidence folder.

---

## 7) Review cadence

Review incident trends monthly; use to influence supplier status in **Supplier_Tracker.md**.
