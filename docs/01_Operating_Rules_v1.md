## 4) Catalogue constraints (Phase 0)

- **3 categories only:** Hygiene Essentials, Mobility & Transfers, Rehab Tech.
- **Minimal SKUs:** curated; quality, documentation, and supplier reliability over breadth.
- **Pricing discipline:** Products selected and priced to align with low-cost strategy and supplier margin viability requirements.

---

## 5) Fulfilment constraints (Phase 0)

- **Dropshipping mandatory:** supplier ships direct to customer.
- **Blind shipping required:** supplier invoice not included in box; customer-facing paperwork is Procura Medical's invoice.
- **Tracking expected** once dispatched.

---

## 6) Returns stance (Phase 0)

- **Change-of-mind returns:** minimized within the law (especially hygiene items).
- **Faults/consumer guarantees:** handled under Australian Consumer Law (ACL); these rights can't be excluded. ([accc.gov.au](https://www.accc.gov.au/consumers/buying-products-and-services/warranties?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

---

## 7) Recordkeeping (Phase 0)

Every order must have:

- an entry in **Order_Log.md**
- supplier documentation stored per **Supplier Tracker / Evidence Pack** requirements
- any incidents recorded in **Incident_Register.md**
- any complaints recorded in **Complaints_Register.md**

---

## 8) Future registration (explicit)

These operating rules are written to support an unregistered provider model in Phase 0 and can be expanded to cover NDIS-registered operations once registration is granted.

### Post-registration switch (when applicable)

When registered (later phase), update:

- Terms + website statements about registration status
- supplier pack to include any additional documentation requirements
- invoicing templates (GST and "Tax Invoice" treatment if GST-registered)
