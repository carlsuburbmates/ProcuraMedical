## Instructions

1. **Category:** Hygiene Essentials / Mobility & Transfers / Rehab Tech
2. **Product:** Product name/model
3. **Target Sell Price:** Intended retail price (AUD)
4. **Candidate Supplier(s):** Supplier name(s) from **Supplier_Tracker.md**
5. **Wholesale Cost:** Cost paid to supplier (AUD)
6. **Shipping Cost:** Approximate cost to ship to customer (AUD, if applicable)
7. **Gross Margin:** Target Sell Price – (Wholesale Cost + Shipping Cost) (AUD)
8. **Review Signal (Evidence):** Link to product review, spec, or clinical guidance supporting selection
9. **Spec Sheet (Evidence):** Link to product specification/datasheet
10. **Returns Risk Notes:** Any notes about hygiene/resale risks (e.g., "sealed only", "non-returnable if opened")
11. **PASS/FAIL:** PASS if margin ≥$200 AND supplier approved; FAIL if margin <$200 OR supplier not approved

---

## Selection Criteria

**PASS:**
- Margin ≥ $200 per unit
- Supplier is in **Supplier_Tracker.md** with status "Approved"
- Product has documented spec/clinical support
- Returns risk is manageable (clearly communicated to customer)

**FAIL:**
- Margin < $200 per unit
- Supplier not approved or status "Pending/Declined"
- No spec/clinical documentation
- Returns risk too high (e.g., opens directly to skin, hygiene concern)

## Launch Sequence (Phase 0 – Feb 11)

### Minimum viable catalogue (day 1):
- 20–30 SKUs across 3 categories
- All from Tier 1 approved suppliers
- All with margin ≥ $200
- All with documented returns risk strategy

### Expansion (Feb–Mar):

- Expand to 50+ SKUs

### Post-Registration (May+):
- Tier 3 suppliers (mid-cost/complex)
- Higher price points ($1,500–$15,000+)
