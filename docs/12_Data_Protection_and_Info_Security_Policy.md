# Data Protection & Information Security Policy (v1.1)

**Version:** v1.1  
**Last updated:** 2026-01-20  
**Applies:** Internal operations

---

## 1) Objective

Protect personal information and operational records.

---

## 2) Data types handled

- customer identity/contact details
- order and invoice records
- plan manager contact details
- support communications
- supplier documentation

---

## 3) Controls

- least-privilege access
- strong authentication/MFA where available
- structured storage aligned to supplier evidence and order logging requirements

---

## 4) Breach handling

Any suspected privacy breach triggers:

- incident entry in **Incident_Register.md**
- containment steps
- assessment and response

---

## 5) Future changes (explicit)

If we add or change major service providers (for example, a new payment gateway or CRM), this policy will be updated to reflect changes in data handling. ([OAIC](https://www.oaic.gov.au/privacy/australian-privacy-principles/australian-privacy-principles-guidelines/chapter-1-app-1-open-and-transparent-management-of-personal-information?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
