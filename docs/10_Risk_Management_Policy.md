# Risk Management Policy (v1.1)

**Version:** v1.1  
**Last updated:** 2026-01-20  
**Applies:** Internal operations

---

## 1) Objective

Reduce operational, compliance, and supplier risks prior to and after going live.

---

## 2) Core risk areas

- supplier reliability (lead times, blind shipping, defects)
- shipping delays/damage
- ACL compliance (returns/faults language) ([accc.gov.au](https://www.accc.gov.au/business/problem-with-a-product-or-service-you-sold/repair-replace-refund-cancel?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
- privacy handling and security ([OAIC](https://www.oaic.gov.au/privacy/australian-privacy-principles/australian-privacy-principles-guidelines/chapter-1-app-1-open-and-transparent-management-of-personal-information?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
- margin viability

---

## 3) Controls (register-aligned)

- **Supplier_Tracker.md** + evidence folder per supplier
- **Order_Log.md** for every order
- **Incident_Register.md** and **Complaints_Register.md** for issues
- monthly review of trends and supplier performance
