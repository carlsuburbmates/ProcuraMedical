## Instructions

1. **Supplier:** Supplier business name
2. **Tier:** 1 (High-volume, low-cost) / 2 (Secondary, low-cost) / 3 (Mid-cost, complex, post-registration)
3. **Status:** Approved / Pending / Declined
4. **Dropship?:** Y/N – confirmed they ship direct to customer
5. **Blind Ship Confirmed?:** Y/N – confirmed no supplier invoice in box
6. **SLA / Lead Time (Written?):** Y/N – dispatch & delivery SLA documented in writing
7. **Returns/Defects Terms (Written?):** Y/N – policy/terms documented in writing
8. **NDIS Codes Provided?:** Y/N – product-to-NDIS code mapping provided
9. **Wholesale Pricing (Version/Date?):** Version number and date of latest pricing list
10. **Margin PASS/FAIL:** PASS if minimum 30% discount from NDIS max prices; FAIL if less
11. **Evidence Folder Location:** Path to supplier folder with SLA, pricing, product catalog, etc.
12. **Next Action:** Next step (e.g., "Awaiting SLA", "Test order placed", "Approved – monitor performance")

---

## Vetting Criteria (Reference)

All **Approved** suppliers must have:

- ✅ Dropshipping capability confirmed
- ✅ Blind shipping confirmed in writing
- ✅ SLA/delivery expectations documented
- ✅ Returns/defects policy documented
- ✅ Wholesale pricing list (with version/date)
- ✅ NDIS code mapping (where applicable)
- ✅ Margin calculation showing ≥30% discount from NDIS max

---

## Status Changes

**Pending → Approved:**
- All vetting criteria met
- Written confirmations received
- Test orders (if applicable) passed

**Approved → Suspended/Under Review:**
- Repeated failures in incident register
- Margin erosion below 30%
- Non-responsive to queries

**Approved → Declined:**
- Unable to meet requirements
- Performance unacceptable

---

## Monthly Review

1. Check **Incident_Register.md** for supplier-linked issues
2. Update status based on incident severity/frequency
3. Note performance changes in "Next Action"
4. Identify if margin has fallen below 30%
