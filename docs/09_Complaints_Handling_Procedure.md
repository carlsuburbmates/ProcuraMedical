# Complaints Handling Procedure (v1.1)

**Version:** v1.1  
**Last updated:** 2026-01-20  
**Applies:** Internal operations

---

## 1) Purpose

A consistent, fair process for customer complaints.

---

## 2) Mandatory register alignment

All complaints must be recorded in **Complaints_Register.md** on receipt.

If linked to an order, reference **Order_Log.md**.

---

## 3) Process

1. acknowledge receipt
2. assess severity and urgency
3. investigate using order/supplier evidence
4. resolve and confirm outcome in writing
5. record closure date and learning notes

---

## 4) Escalation triggers

- safety risk allegation
- ACL remedy dispute escalation
- repeated supplier issues (update **Supplier_Tracker.md**)
