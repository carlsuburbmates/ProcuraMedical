# Participant Safeguarding Policy (v1.1)

**Version:** v1.1  
**Last updated:** 2026-01-20  
**Applies:** Internal operations

---

## 1) Commitment

We commit to respectful, safe, and dignified service.

---

## 2) Boundaries

We supply products and operational support; we do not replace clinical assessment.

---

## 3) Risk reduction measures

- curated catalogue + documentation expectations
- incident + complaints systems in place
- escalation pathway for safety concerns

---

## 4) Reporting

Any safeguarding concern is handled via **Incident_Management_Procedure.md** and recorded in **Incident_Register.md**.
