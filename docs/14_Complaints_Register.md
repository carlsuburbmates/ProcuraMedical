## Instructions

1. **Complaint ID:** Assign sequential number (CMP-001, CMP-002, etc.)
2. **Date Received:** Date complaint was received (YYYY-MM-DD)
3. **Channel:** Email / Phone / Chat / Contact Form
4. **Order ID:** Link to **Order_Log.md** entry (ORD-XXXX) if applicable
5. **Summary:** 1–2 line description of complaint
6. **Severity:** L (Low) / M (Moderate) / H (High)
7. **Action Taken:** Steps taken to investigate/resolve
8. **Outcome:** Resolution or status
9. **Closed Date:** Date complaint was closed (YYYY-MM-DD)
10. **Notes:** Any additional context (ACL rights asserted, dispute, escalation, etc.)

---

## Escalation Triggers

Flag for immediate escalation if:
- Safety risk alleged
- ACL remedy dispute
- Repeated complaints from same customer
- Systemic supplier issue

---

## Monthly Review

Review all complaints from the prior month to identify:
- Patterns (e.g., recurring issues with specific supplier/product)
- Severity trends
- ACL remedy claims and outcomes
- Supplier performance impact → update **Supplier_Tracker.md**
